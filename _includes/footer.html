bio

I am an artist and technologist with over a decade of experience in both commercial digital products and conceptual art.

I am from Baltimore, Maryland but currently split time between Mexico City and New York City.

I like endurance sports and fashion but don't want to talk about either with anyone from the internet. You can learn more about me and my other interests on my homepage.

artist statement

I have a lot of loud and malleable opinions about technology, politics, and economics. I have mostly admiration for the arts (not including the art market or academia!) and don't really waste talking about art I don't enjoy. I tend to make art that is in homage to the creative works I love and a challenge to the things out in the real world I find frustrating. I'm particularly interested in exploring ideas about identity and sexuality in relationship to the rapidly moving target of digital life.

I like paintings and video games and want to make more of both.
