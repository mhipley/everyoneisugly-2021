bio

I am an artist and technologist with over a decade of experience in both commercial digital products and conceptual art.

I am from Baltimore, Maryland but currently split time between Mexico City and New York City.

I like endurance sports and fashion but don't want to talk about either with anyone from the internet. You can learn more about me and my other interests on my homepage.

artist statement

A lot of artists say they can't imagine themselves doing anything but making art. I think this is kind of bullshit. I've had office jobs for most of my adult life and have spent the better part of a decade working in both large corporations and small startups in the technology sector.

I am the person at the art party who has a lot of opinions about "apps" and actually understands how they work. I make work about my preoccupations with technology and the people who make it, about the extended adolescence of a life forever-online, about feeling stuck between a corporate world I loathe and an art world that I also don't really vibe with.

I like paintings and video games and want to make more of both.
